Mona Lisa by Leonardo Da Vinci<br/>Everyone will be agreed if The Mona Lisa is the world’s most famous painting. This painting is owned by the French government and hangs in the Louvre in Paris. The painting shows a woman looking out at the viewer with what is often described as an “enigmatic smile”. The Mona Lisa is perhaps the most famous piece in art history. If Mona Lisa sold, it will be the most expensive painting in the world ever, or maybe the most expensive art works in human history.
