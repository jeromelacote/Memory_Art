The Turquoise Marilyn by Andy Warhol<br/>The Turquoise Marilyn (1964) is one of the five paintings of Marilyn Monroe created in 1964 by the American Pop Artist Andy Warhol (1928-1987). The paintings are square-shaped with sides measuring 40 inches, and painted with different colored backgrounds of turquoise, light blue, sage blue, red and orange for each work.
