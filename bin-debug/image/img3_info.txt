Composition by Kazimir Malevich<br/>"The Suprematist Composition (Blue rectangle over the red beam) is an oil painting by the Russian painter Kazimir Malevich (1879-1935).  The painting depicts a rare combination of geometrical figures and bright colors arranged harmoniously in space.
Created in 1916, the Suprematist Composition was exhibited in Moscow in 1919-20. In 1927, Malevich exhibited the work at exhibitions in Warsaw, Munich and later at the Grosse Berliner Kunstausstellung in Berlin. These exhibitions brought him international fame and recognition of his artistic style. But abruptly he had to return to the Soviet Union (USSR) in June 1927 when he was summoned home."
